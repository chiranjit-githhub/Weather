About AtmosCity

AtmosCity is a one-stop platform designed to provide comprehensive city-related information. It brings together real-time weather and air quality updates, tourist attraction details, local cuisine and restaurant recommendations, and helpful language tips for travelers. Whether you're planning your next adventure or exploring your own city, AtmosCity offers a seamless experience.

Team: Chiranjit Sahu and Akshay Thakur
Motivation: To create a user-friendly platform that enhances urban exploration and travel planning by leveraging modern technology.
